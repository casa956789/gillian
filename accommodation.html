Our Luxurious Rooms & Facilities

At Gillian Moore's B&B, we pride ourselves on providing comfortable and elegantly furnished, non-smoking rooms designed for your relaxation. Each room is thoughtfully equipped to ensure a pleasant stay.

Private Bathrooms with Tropical Rain Showers

All our rooms feature private bathrooms with full-size baths and invigorating walk-in tropical rain showers, complemented by beautiful wooden floors.

Comfort & Amenities

Enjoy modern comforts including central heating, an iron and ironing board, hair dryer, a 40" flat T.V., and tea/coffee making facilities in every room.

Delicious Breakfast

Start your day right with our full, hearty breakfast, prepared fresh daily. Breakfast is served from 8:00 AM on weekdays and 7:30 AM on weekends, ensuring you're energized for your adventures in Galway.
