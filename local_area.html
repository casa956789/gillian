Discover Galway & Beyond

Gillian Moore's B&B is ideally located for exploring the vibrant city of Galway and the stunning natural beauty of the surrounding region.

Galway City Highlights

Just a short stroll away, you'll find Eyre Square Shopping Centre, two beautiful sandy beaches, and spectacular coastal walks. Immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere of traditional Irish music pubs and enjoy a wide variety of restaurants.

Activities & Adventures

For the active traveler, horse-riding, golfing, fishing, and various water sports are readily available. Bikes can also be hired for exploring at your own pace.

Iconic Sightseeing Tours

Embark on unforgettable sightseeing tours to Connemara, the majestic Cliffs of Moher, and the enchanting Aran Islands. All tickets for these tours can be conveniently purchased upon your arrival at the B&B.

Getting Here & Around

Our B&B is approximately 40 miles (80 km) from Shannon Airport and about a 2-hour drive from Dublin Airport. Taxis are available on request, and for your convenience, the nearest bus stop is just 50 yards away, providing easy access to local attractions.
